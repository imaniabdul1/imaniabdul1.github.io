"Foliage Finance"

iOS App - 2024

Foliage Finance is an innovative and engaging financial education platform designed for teenagers, young adults, and those who haven’t received adequate financial education. The app features a budgeting screen to manage expenses, a tax filing simulation with W2 and 1040 forms, and a budgeting game that incorporates real-world financial scenarios. It also includes financial dashboards to track real-life debts, a credit literacy lesson and quiz, and a budgeting quiz. Foliage Finance was recognized as a finalist in the top 4 out of 43 teams in an app competition, highlighting its effectiveness and appeal in empowering users to master personal finance.

"A.D.A Budgeting App"

Web App - 2024

A budgeting app designed specifically for teenagers and students, featuring a budgeting calendar to track income and expenses, a savings calculator to help set and achieve financial goals, and an array of educational resources on topics like budgeting, saving, and managing money responsibly. The app empowers young users to develop strong financial habits early, offering easy-to-understand tools and tips tailored to their needs, with an emphasis on real-world application and financial literacy.

"Victorian Ballroom"

Website - 2023

A Choose Your Own Adventure (CYOA) game featuring historically accurate choices. about Victorian fashion, ballroom etiquette, and class distinctions. Players will navigate the complex social dynamics of the era, making decisions about attire, behavior, and social interactions to survive a night in the Victorian ballroom.

"Help Muslim Women in India"

Website - 2022

An Activist Website dedicated to shedding light on the challenges and resilience of Muslim women in India. The website serves as an educational resource through detailed articles, multimedia content, and interactive features. The website raises awareness about issues such as discrimination, gender inequality, and religious persecution, while also celebrating the strength and cultural contributions of Muslim women. It encourages users to share resources and participate in advocacy efforts aimed at creating a positive change.

"Are You a Moth or a Butterfly?"

Website - 2022

A personality quiz that uses color preferences, activity levels, and time-of-day habits from answer choices to decide if the user is most like a moth or butterfly.
